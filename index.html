About Nodeschool

Nodeschool helps people learn node.js (and javascript) through hands on workshops both online and offline.

There is a collection of interactive workshops on the nodeschool website which you can work through - either from your desk or by joining an event where you get to work together with other people and request real time mentoring from others.

There are no upcoming offline events. You can join us on Github for online discussions.
